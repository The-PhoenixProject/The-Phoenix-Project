How to Recycle Properly

1

Clean Your Items

Rinse containers to remove food residue. Dirty items can contaminate entire batches of recyclables.

2

Check Local Guidelines

Recycling rules vary by location. Check with your local recycling program for specific requirements.

3

Sort Materials

Separate different types of materials (paper, plastic, glass, metal) if required by your program.

4

Don't Bag Recyclables

Place items loose in your recycling bin. Plastic bags can jam sorting equipment.
